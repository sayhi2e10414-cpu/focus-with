## 中文发布动态

> 我把自己一直在用的专注系统整理成开源项目了。
>
> 它会管理方向、项目和任务，记录每天与每周的专注时间，也可以连接 Claude、Telegram、iPhone 快捷指令和 macOS 悬浮计时器。

> 最重要的是：它默认只属于你自己。不开 AI 也能完整使用；如果想让 AI 参与，它只能通过明确的工具和权限帮你开始、回来、完成，再选择下一步。
>
> 它叫 FocusWith。希望它不是又一个要求你自律的工具，而是真的陪你把事情做完。

## English launch post

> I turned the focus system I use for myself into an open-source project.
>
> FocusWith organizes directions, projects, and tasks; tracks daily and weekly focus; and optionally connects to Claude, Telegram, iPhone Shortcuts, and a native macOS floating timer.
>
> Most importantly, it is private by default. It works without AI, and when an AI is connected, it acts through a small, explicit set of tools.
>
> I hope it feels less like another app asking you to be disciplined, and more like something that stays with you until the work is done.
